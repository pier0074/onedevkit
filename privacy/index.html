Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to OneDevKit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle information when you use our website and tools at onedevkit.com (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Data You Enter Into Our Tools

We do not collect, store, or transmit any data you enter into our tools. All processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Your JSON data, passwords, UUIDs, and any other content you generate stays on your device.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may collect limited technical information:

  • Analytics data: If you consent to cookies, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics (pages visited, time on site, general location, device type). This helps us understand how to improve our tools.
  • Server logs: Our hosting provider (Cloudflare) may collect standard web server logs including IP addresses, browser type, and pages accessed for security and performance purposes.

Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal cookies and local storage:

  • Cookie consent: We store your cookie preference in local storage to remember your choice.
  • Theme preference: Your dark/light mode preference is stored locally.
  • Analytics cookies: Only if you accept cookies, Google Analytics may set cookies to track anonymous usage patterns.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve our tools and user experience
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Remember your preferences (theme, cookie consent)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Cloudflare

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect technical data for security, performance, and analytics. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Google Analytics

If you consent to analytics cookies, we use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage statistics. You can opt out by declining cookies or using browser extensions like Google Analytics Opt-out. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

  • Tool data: Never stored - all processing is client-side
  • Analytics data: Retained by Google Analytics per their data retention settings (typically 14-26 months)
  • Local storage: Stored in your browser until you clear it

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

For All Users

  • Decline analytics cookies when prompted
  • Clear your browser's cookies and local storage at any time
  • Use browser privacy settings or extensions to block tracking

For EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

For California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access. Since we don't collect or store your tool data, there's nothing sensitive to breach. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all connections.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us.
