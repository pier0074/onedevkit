## After Generation
1. Save images as PNG format
2. Optimize with TinyPNG or similar (aim for <100KB each)
3. Place in `/images/og/` folder:
   - `og-default.png`
   - `json-formatter.png`
   - `password-generator.png`
   - `uuid-generator.png`
   - `lorem-ipsum.png`
   - `qr-code-generator.png`
